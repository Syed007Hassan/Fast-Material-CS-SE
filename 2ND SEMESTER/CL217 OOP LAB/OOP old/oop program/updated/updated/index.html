About The Conference

National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, Karachi campus in collaboration with IEEE, Karachi intents to come up with an International Conference on Emerging Trends in Smart Technologies(ICETST) 2020. It is an integrative platform for researchers, scientists, practitioners, Industrialists, academicians and students to extant their contributions related to the emerging applications, future trends, advancements, innovations and experiences in the areas of computing, mathematics and engineering technologies.
